About Us

La Cocina de Mamá began as a humble family kitchen and has grown into East Harlem’s beloved spot for authentic Mexican comfort food. We honour our heritage by preparing everything from scratch – from grinding corn for our tortillas to simmering our salsas for hours. Every dish is a tribute to the recipes passed down through generations.

Customers rave about our al pastor tacos, fish tacos and quesadillas, lovingly prepared with the freshest ingredients. We take pride in serving generous portions at reasonable prices, ensuring that every visitor leaves full and happy. Our hospitable team treats guests like family, creating a warm ambiance reminiscent of dining in someone’s home.

When you dine with us, you’re not just eating a meal – you’re experiencing a piece of Mexico in New York City. We invite you to savour our culinary traditions and become part of our extended family.

Why We’re Unique

  • Handmade Tortillas: Every tortilla is pressed and cooked in‑house for a tender, flavourful base.
  • Family Recipes: Our dishes are derived from secret family recipes with ingredients sourced locally whenever possible.
  • Generous Portions & Fair Prices: We believe everyone should enjoy great Mexican food without breaking the bank.
  • Warm Hospitality: Our staff greets you like an old friend and ensures you feel right at home.
